About Me

Introduction

  My name is Yu Yuan (袁煜， In Chinese). I am currently persuing my Ms.c degree from Department of Aerospace Information and Control at Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Zhongliang Jing . My research interests include computer vision, image processing and machine learning. I obtained my B.S. degree at from School of Aeronautics and Astronautics at Shanghai Jiao Tong University in June 2020.

Interests

Photography

  So far, I have more than ten years of photography experience, mainly focusing on landscape photography and starfield photography. You can find some of my photography here. I also have experience in video shooting and production.

Drones

  I am skilled in the operation of rotor drones (UAV), and have taken aerial photos of important activities of Shanghai Jiao Tong University for many times. Meanwhile, I also have certain experience in the design and production of drones.

Flight and flight simulation

  I am an avid flyer. So far, I have experienced more than 30 types of aircraft in real life, including various mainline, regional and general aviation aircraft. I'm also an accomplished user of Prepar3D, Microsoft Flight Simulator 2020 and other flight simulation platforms.

Swimming, Cycling, Hiking, Rowing, and Others
